Established in 2025, Nox and Noon is a dual-vision imprint dedicated to publishing high-quality fantasy literature across a spectrum of voices and traditions. We are committed to elevating work that blends imaginative storytelling with literary craft, with a particular focus on supporting Latino authors within the genre.


FAN POLICY:

The author and Nox and Noon Press retain all copyrights in their Creative Works. This means no copies, distributions, derivative works, and public displays. A “derivative work” is a work based on one or more of a Nox and Noon Press property. Nox and Noon does not permit public displays of any text or art from any of their works. Any license or permission to use any intellectual property from Nox and Noon Press, or permission to claim that fan product is “official,” MUST BE IN WRITING and is otherwise invalid.

POLÍTICA PARA FANS:

El autor y Nox and Noon Press conservan todos los derechos de autor de sus Obras Creativas. Esto significa que no se permiten copias, distribuciones, obras derivadas ni exhibiciones públicas. Una "obra derivada" es una obra basada en una o más de las propiedades de Nox and Noon Press. Nox and Noon no permite exhibiciones públicas de ningún texto ni arte de sus obras. Cualquier licencia o permiso para usar cualquier propiedad intelectual de Nox and Noon Press, o permiso para afirmar que un producto de fans es "oficial", DEBE CONSTITUIRSE POR ESCRITO y, en caso contrario, no será válido.
